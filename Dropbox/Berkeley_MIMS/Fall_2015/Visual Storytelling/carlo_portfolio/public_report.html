Public Report:

Data Visualization for Hawaii's Education

[Data Visualization] [R] [RStudio]







In conjunction with the lead data analyst and the System Evaluation and Reporting Section (SERS) team, I lead the effort to develop data visualization pieces for each of the 286 Hawaii Department of Education schools in accordance with the new Elementary and Secondary Education Act (ESEA).

I developed the code in R which will be used for all the future annual reports. Moreover, the iterative approach refined the structure of the assessment data pipeline. I designed for simplicity without losing any critical information, as the report demanded consideration for a gamut of interested stakeholders—students, principals, community members, and non-profit organizations. It was rewarding to employ my technical skills grounded in a human-centered approach and collaborative environment.
