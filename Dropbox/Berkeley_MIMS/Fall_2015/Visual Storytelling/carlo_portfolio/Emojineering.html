Emojineering:

Is it possible to predict emoji use?

[Natural Language Processing] [Python] [Machine Learning] [Scikit-Learn] [Web-Scraping]







The goal of the project will be to create an emoji tool that will create useful semantic categories of emojis and suggest relevant emojis for a given document. It is well documented that emojis and emoticons can express meaning in our texting and social media posting in ways that simple words cannot. We will analyze why texts contain emojis and why others do not. We will answer questions like: Is there substantive semantic value in these emojis? Can we also extract sentiment? The final step of the process will be to classify the category of emoji, where an emoji is appropriate.
