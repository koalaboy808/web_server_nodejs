Find'NRide:

Whatever your hunger, we provide the information to get you there

[Web Development] [Django] [Postgres] [Heroku] [Javascript]







Group project with other graduate students from the School of Information to develop a web application that queries the Yelp API for a food item. With just a single keyword, we implement a combination of Google and Uber APIs to provide the the user a list of options nearby. Bus, foot, Uber, cycle, or car--the choice is up to you! We provide the data-rich platform. You get your meal.



We built this using Django, Postgres, and Heroku. I was actively involved in the entire full stack development process. I specficially tackled the problem of handling the Google and Uber APIs.



Group Members: Brian Goodness, Keshav Potluri, Richa Prajapati
